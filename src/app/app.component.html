About us
Bonsai Capital provides boutique investment banking services, bringing together Israeli technology companies and international equity, financial and strategic in nature. Bonsai Capital's services include raising growth funds for Israel-based technology companies and scouting investment and M&A opportunities for international firms, financial & strategic.

 Bonsai Capital maintains and serves a large investor community, based primarily in North America, China, Japan, and Australia, with local partners and affiliates based in Hong-Kong, Japan, and Australia.

 The company's strength lies in its team's ability to screen attractive opportunities for investment & acquisitions and guide its technology clients through the complex process of raising funds or selling their company.
